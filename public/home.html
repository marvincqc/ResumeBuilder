Common questions.

Straight answers about how JobReady works.

Can each overseas partner have its own link?

Yes. Each partner gets a unique link with their agency tag pre-attached.

Is the Singapore agency preselected?

Yes. The target agency is locked in from the partner link — no manual typing needed.

Can workers import an old resume?

Yes. Browser OCR reads a resume image and pre-fills the form. Workers can attach extra files too.

Where does the submission go?

It's saved to the agency folder and mirrored to storage — with the partner tag included.
